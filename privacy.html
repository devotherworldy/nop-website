# NOP Privacy Policy **Last Updated:** July 20, 2026 ## 1. Information We Collect NOP stores only the information required to operate the moderation network, including: * Discord User IDs * Discord Server IDs * Server Names * Report reasons * Evidence links provided by moderators * Report status (Pending, Verified, or False) * Reviewer User IDs * Dates and timestamps NOP does not collect passwords, private messages, email addresses, payment information, or any information outside of Discord that is not voluntarily submitted through the bot. ## 2. How We Use Your Information Collected information is used to: * Process moderation reports. * Verify reports submitted by participating servers. * Help participating communities identify users with verified reports. * Maintain moderation logs and case history. * Improve the security and reliability of the NOP network. ## 3. Data Sharing Verified moderation information may be shared with servers that use NOP. Pending reports, declined reports, and internal moderation records are only accessible to authorised NOP staff unless required by law. ## 4. Data Retention Reports may be retained for as long as necessary to operate the network, investigate appeals, prevent abuse, and maintain historical moderation records. ## 5. Your Rights Server owners or affected users may contact the NOP moderation team to request a review of a verified report or appeal a moderation decision. ## 6. Security NOP takes reasonable steps to protect stored information but cannot guarantee absolute security. ## 7. Changes This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of NOP after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
