NOP Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 20, 2026

1. Information We Collect

NOP stores only the information required to operate the moderation network. This includes Discord User IDs, Server IDs, report information, evidence provided by users, and moderation decisions.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected by NOP is used to process reports, verify moderation cases, maintain case history, and help participating Discord communities protect their members.

3. Data Sharing

Verified reports may be shared with servers participating in the NOP network. Internal review information is only accessible to authorised NOP staff.

4. Contact

If you have questions or want to appeal a report, contact the NOP moderation team through our official Discord server.
